## Tips for Generation
- Use aspect ratio **2:3** for all card designs (standard playing card proportions)
- Request **PNG with transparent backgrounds** for UI elements and icons
- Specify **high resolution (at least 1024x1536 for cards)** for quality
- Maintain **consistent art style** across all cards (same outline weight, similar color saturation)
- Include **bold black outlines** for that comic/cartoon aesthetic
- Keep designs **clear and recognizable** even at smaller sizes

---

## Recommended AI Tools
- **Midjourney**: Best for stylized, artistic card illustrations
- **DALL-E 3**: Great for specific, detailed prompts with consistent style
- **Stable Diffusion (with ControlNet)**: For precise control over composition
- **Adobe Firefly**: Good for commercial-safe, high-quality illustrations
